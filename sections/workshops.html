During recent years, I have been teaching and organizing different workshops and other open spaces to dialogue and learn about journalism and to do journalism. The main topics of my workshops have been Ethics and Journalism, Access to Public Information as a tool for investigative journalism, Right to Information and Tools for Digital Journalism.

Some of the activities include workshops with the Knight Center for Journalism in the Americas, Periodistas de a Pie and Red de Periodistas de Juárez, among other, and the collaboration to organize Migrahack México, and the Foro Latinoamericano de Medios Digitales y Periodismo.
