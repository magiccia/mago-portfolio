During the last years, I have been teaching and organizing different workshops and other open spaces to dialague and learn about journalism and to do journalism. The main topics of my workshops have been Ethics and Journalism, Access to Public Information as a tool for investigative journalism, Right to Information and Tools for Digital Journalism.

Some of the activities include workshops with the Red de Periodistas de Juarez, Periodistas de a Pie and Programa Prensa y Democracia, and the collaboration to organize events like Migrahack Mexico, and the Foro Latinoamericano de Medios Digitales y Periodismo.
