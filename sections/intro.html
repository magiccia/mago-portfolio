I am Margarita Torres PhD, a full-time scholar in the Department of Communication at Universidad Iberoamericana, where I teach in the undergraduate program in Communication, in the Master in Communication Program and in the PhD Communication Program. I coordinate the journalism area, the Right to Information Program and the Iberoamerican Journal of Communication. I am also a cofounder of Periodistas de a Pie, a network of journalists based in Mexico City, mainly dedicated to the training of journalists with a human rights perspective.

My work is focused on the research, discussion and application of the right to information as the main approach. This approach implies the connection between journalism, academia and civic society. This includes topics as ethics in journalism and digital journalism combining access to public information, technology and open data. The final purpose to achieve is to empower citizens' participation for a better democracy.
