I am Margarita Torres PhD, a full-time scholar in the Communication Department at Universidad Iberoamericana, where I teach in the undergraduate program in Communication, in the Master in Communication Program and in the PhD Communication Program. I coordinate the journalism area, the Right to Information Program and the Iberoamerican Journal of Communication. I am also a cofounder of Periodistas de a Pie, a network of journalists based in Mexico City, dedicated mainly to the training of journalists with a human rights perspective.

My work focuses on the research, discussion and application of the right to information that connects journalism, academia and civic society, from ethics in journalism to digital journalism, crossing the access to public information, technology and open data. The final purpose: to empower citizen participation for a better democracy.
